MATERIAL DOCUMENTS REQUIRED TO PROCESS BUILDING PERMIT

The following materials are required for the processing of building permits.

It is important to have all the documents ready in other to get a permit.


The requirements include the following;
- 5 sets of architectural drawings
- 5 sets of structural drawing (to include letter of supervision and calculation sheets).
- 5 sets of electrical drawings
- 5 sets of mechanical drawings

- Evidence of land ownership/title document, which could be:
  * Stamped duty land purchase reciept;
  * Letter of ratification; and;
  * Certificate of occupancy (C of O)/Land certificate
  * Governor’s consent
  * 1 copy of sunprint survey plan;

- Current tax clearance certificate;
- Sworn affidavit in lieu of Tenement Rate;
- 2 copies of environmental technical analysis report to be prepared by a Registered Town Planner where applicable;
- Assessment fee and other government reciept(s);
- Police Report
- Fire Service report iin case of applications for Petrol/Gas Filling stations and high rise buildigs

- For Company and Corporate bodies;
  * Company Tax Clearance (CTC)
  * Two (2) Directors Tax Clearance;
  * Evidence of PAYE returns; and
  * Certificate of Incorporation.

*Ensure you have all required documents before proceeding
to applying for a building permit if not contact support
